Privacy Policy

  • 1. Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

  • Importance of privacy and confidentiality
  • Disclosure of personal information

2. Applicability of the Privacy Policy

  • This applied to any person who purchases or intends to purchase any product or service from Travbox
  • All sales channels include a website, mobile site, mobile application, web application, company office or call center, etc.

3. Non-Applicability of the Privacy Policy

  • Excludes third-party websites, mobile applications, web applications, or mobile sites from its purview
  • Advises users to review the respective privacy policies of other websites before using them

4. Geographical Limits and Jurisdiction

  • Personal data and information primarily used in India and other geographical locations and jurisdictions where third parties engaged with Travbox process it on their behalf
  • Users agree to Travbox using their personal data and information for the purposes mentioned in the privacy policy as per data protection regulations in India

5. Information Collected and Its Legality

  • Personal information including name, age, gender, nationality, religion, marital status, etc.
  • Contact details include mobile number, email address, postal address, telephone number, fax, etc
  • Banking details including account number, debit/credit card details, net banking details, UPI id, etc.
  • Transaction details excluding banking information to understand visitors’ and users’ eCommerce activities and behavior.
  • Usernames, passwords, email addresses, and security details are entered for your Travbox account to avail of services.
  • Users voluntarily store data, media files, and other documents on Travbox servers.
  • Any data related to users that is available in the public domain or is received by Travbox from any third-party channels.
  • Information for any third party or traveler for whom users made a booking on Travbox.
  • Copies of passports, bank statements, original application forms, photographs, and other documents or information as necessary to further the visa process.
  • PAN information and passport details in compliance with the Liberalised Remittance Scheme of RBI and other laws for international trips and bookings.
  • Copies of Government identification like Aadhar, Driving Licence, Voter ID, etc., and other details may be required for bookings at hotels on places of stay during the trip.
  • Covid-19 Vaccination status and certificate where such information is required.

6. Use of Collected Information

  • All information is kept strictly confidential and used solely for the purposes mentioned in the privacy policy.
  • May share information with third-party for bookings and other required purposes.
  • Users can opt out of surveys and promotional messages and emails at any time.

7. Retention of Personal Information

  • Personal data and information retained for a reasonable period
  • Safely deleted from servers when no longer required.

8. Cookies Policy

  • Use of cookies to personalize user experience on the website.
